Personal Project · In Development

Chivito

A Puerto Rico-focused product concept for discovering trusted local professionals and services.

RoleProduct strategy, UI/UX & development
FocusLocal service discovery
AudiencePeople and providers in Puerto Rico
StatusConcept in development
01 · Opportunity

Finding the right local provider often depends on fragmented recommendations.

People frequently rely on social posts, group chats, or word of mouth to find nearby services. Useful recommendations exist, but they can be difficult to search, compare, and revisit.

02 · Product vision

Create a trusted, Puerto Rico-first discovery experience.

Chivito is being explored as a focused directory that can help people discover local professionals while giving independent providers a clearer way to present their work.

  • Search and browse by service category and location.
  • Make provider information easy to compare and contact.
  • Use trust signals that help people make informed decisions.
  • Build a visual identity that feels unmistakably connected to Puerto Rico.
03 · Current stage

Brand and product direction are established; validation comes next.

The project is still in development. The next responsible step is to validate the most useful service categories and trust signals with potential users and local providers before locking in the technical architecture.

04 · Next steps

Move from concept to a focused minimum viable product.

  • Interview users and service providers.
  • Prototype search, profiles, and contact flows.
  • Test the product with a small geographic and service-category scope.
  • Select the implementation stack after the core workflow is validated.
